About the Founder

At the heart of Investo Grow is the vision and expertise of its founder, Nitin Jain, a seasoned professional with over 25 years of sales experience and 6 years of dedicated expertise in the real estate sector.

Nitin’s journey into real estate began with his own investments. Through firsthand experience, he recognized the immense potential this sector holds for wealth creation when approached with the right strategy and guidance.
